The Product

What is Minority Report?

Minority Report is a multi-agent AI system that continuously monitors commercial signals across Modern Trade, computes a real-time sell-out risk score, and autonomously routes alerts and ranked recommendations to the right decision-makers — before volume decline becomes visible in actuals.
👁
DETECT
Early Warning
Synthesises POS, distribution, pricing, brand and macro signals into a single 0–100 risk score updated every week.
🔔
ALERT
Right person, right time
Severity-graded alerts routed to Country MD, Commercial Director, Supply Chain — through Teams, email, or push.
💡
RECOMMEND
Ranked Actions
AI-generated corrective actions with ROI estimates, effort scores, owner assignment, and KPI targets.
LEARN
Closed-loop feedback
Every action taken (or not taken) is recorded. The model tracks outcome vs prediction, reweights signal importance, and improves with each 12-week cycle. This is how the system earns trust progressively with Country MDs.

Why agents, not a monolith?

1
Independent deployability
Each agent can be upgraded (e.g., swap Risk Detection for a new ML model) without touching the rest of the pipeline.
2
Auditable reasoning
Every agent logs its decision trace. Country MDs can inspect exactly why a risk score is 72 — satisfying Responsible AI requirements.
3
Handles conflicting models gracefully
MMM and pricing tools feed into the Ingestion Agent as additional signals — not replacements. Conflicts are surfaced, not hidden.

Key insight on data quality
Imperfect data ≠ no intelligence
Each source is weighted by its freshness and quality score. Low-quality signals (BTL, Macro) contribute less to the composite risk score, and uncertainty is made transparent — not hidden behind a clean number.

This is what makes Minority Report trustworthy in a world where data is "uneven across sources" — the system knows what it doesn't know.

How the Risk Score is Computed

The Risk Detection Agent evaluates 5 independent risk dimensions, each scored 0–100, then combines them using a weighted composite formula. A data-quality uncertainty penalty is added when source quality degrades.

Composite formula
Weighted sum + quality penalty
Score = POS(0.30) + Dist(0.25) + Price(0.22)
       + Brand(0.13) + Macro(0.10)
       + (1 − dataQuality) × 8 Each dimension is individually interpretable, making the score auditable for Responsible AI compliance.
Severity thresholds
4-tier escalation model
CRITICAL (75–100): All-hands escalation, push alerts
HIGH (55–74): MD + Commercial + Supply Chain
MEDIUM (35–54): Commercial Director + Field Sales
LOW (0–34): Field Sales Manager only
Model evolution path
Heuristic → ML → LLM-augmented
Pilot uses a calibrated heuristic model (fast to deploy, fully explainable). As outcome data accumulates over 3–4 cycles, a gradient-boosted model replaces it. LLM augmentation for recommendation narrative quality comes at scale.

Responsible AI

Built for Trust, Not Just Performance

Country MDs have explicitly asked for Responsible AI clarity before committing to rollout. Minority Report is designed from the ground up to be explainable, auditable, and human-supervised — not a black box.

🔍
Full Explainability
Every risk score shows its component breakdown. The agent reasoning log is stored and auditable. No score without an explanation.
👤
Human-in-the-Loop
All recommendations require human review before execution. The system advises — people decide. This is policy, not just a disclaimer.
⚖️
Bias Monitoring
Outcome tracking flags when the model systematically over- or under-predicts risk for a market — triggering a model review.
🔒
Data Governance
PII-free signal sources. Data lineage tracked per ingestion run. GDPR-compatible by design. Retailer data never leaves the OpCo boundary.
📋
Uncertainty Transparency
Low-quality signals inflate the uncertainty band on the risk score. The system shows confidence, not false precision.
🔄
Model Drift Detection
Prediction vs actuals tracked weekly. If model accuracy drops below threshold, automated alert fires to the Chapter Lead for review.
"Responsible AI is not a constraint on speed — it's a precondition for adoption. Country MDs will not deploy what they cannot explain to their board."
— Design principle, Minority Report v1.0
